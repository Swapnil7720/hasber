HASBER c-30

The HASBER C-30 is a fully automatic truck mounted mobile batch-mix plant cum mobile concrete mixer. It can produce and also transport Concrete, thus HASBER C-30 is a combination of a batching plant and a transit mixer. The C-30 machine is powered by a KOEL slave engine which is not used during transport and thus saves a lot of fuel. It is provided with a Twin shaft Mixer duly protected under patent laws and is able to produce a Homogeneous Concrete Mix within seconds. It has separate bins for three aggregates, two cements, water & admixture and is provided with a fully automatic PLC Control Panel on a 28 Tonne Truck Chassis.

HASBER C-30 is provided with-

  • Three separate bins for three types of aggregates such as- 10 mm, 20mm and sand with three separate conveyors.
  • Two separate bins for two cementitious materials such as Cement and Fly ash or GGBS with two separate conveyors.
  • Two adjoined water compartments with a heavy duty water pump
  • Two admixture tank with admixture pump.
  • Separate Compartment for mineral Admixture – Micro silica.

HASBER C-30 is provided with a heavy duty hydraulic drive from the said In-built Engine PTO/ a separate slave engine which enables simultaneous discharge of the ingredients into the Twin shaft mixer in required quantities to produce a homogeneous concrete mix in a very short cycle time of 60 seconds. C-30 is provided with a fully automatic batching system based on VWT (Weight, Volume & Time) batching system (patented). The Twin Shaft Mixer provided is a high torque WCMD Mixer without weighing function which is protected by a granted Indian Patent. The mixer is provided with a hydraulic discharge gate.
