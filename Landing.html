Highlights Of HASBER

HASBER C-30 is a highly compact and Portable RMC PRODUCTION unit along with the Transportation equipment suitable for installation on a 28 Ton Truck Chassis.

HASBER C-30 is provided with a high torque TWIN SHAFT MIXER (Indian patent granted) with an external drive assembly supported on a steel fabricated structure with a batch capacity of 0.35 cubic meter of wet compacted concrete per batch.

FAQ’s

For the first time in India, HASBER offers C-30 & C-30 plus Mobile Concrete Mixers which produce as well as transport concrete. The C-30 & C-30 plus machines are patented in INDIA and have been granted multiple patents.

No. The C-30 & C-30 plus machines batch all the materials on “Weight” basis. Wherein the VWT weight batching system is separately patented in INDIA..

The C-30 and C-30 plus Mobile Concrete Mixers are provided with three separate bins for three separate aggregates namely 10 mm stone, 20 mm stone and Sand. The C-30 and C-30 plus mixers also are provided with two separate bins for cement & fly ash (or GGBS). Unlike any other equipment in India, the C-30 & C-30 plus mixers are also provided with two large capacity water tanks, one on each side of the truck enough to produce concrete even at a remote location. The Model C-30 is provided with single admixture and Model C-30 is provided with two admixtures and additional dosing system such as micro-silica.
